Team Pop Power

Meet Team Pop Power — a dodgeball dream team made up of bold, confident, and unstoppable icons! From pop stars to superheroes, these teammates bring the perfect mix of style, energy, and power to dominate the court.

Hannah Montana

As the team leader, Hannah brings confidence, rhythm, and a rockstar attitude. She’s great at keeping everyone motivated — and she knows how to dodge anything thrown her way, both in music and in dodgeball.

Lava Girl

With her fiery energy and blazing speed, Lava Girl is the team’s unstoppable force. She brings heat to every match and makes sure no one gets left behind.

Shark Boy

Shark Boy is fast, fierce, and always ready to dive into action. His quick reflexes and fearless attitude make him a vital part of Team Pop Power.

Lizzie McGuire

Lizzie adds charm and creativity to the team. She’s quick-thinking, fun, and always ready with a clever dodge move — even if it’s slightly awkward.

Barbie

Barbie is stylish but fierce. Don’t let the pink fool you — she’s organized, athletic, and ready to turn every match into a win with her confidence and strategy.
