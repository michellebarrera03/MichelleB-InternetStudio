About This Collection

Welcome to my personal movie collection and now your new movie list! These are 12 films that have left a lasting impact on me. From heartwarming animations to mind-bending thrillers, each poster here represents a story that made me laugh, cry, or sit on the edge of my seat. I've included my honest ratings and thoughts on what makes each one special.

Lock Stock & 2 Smoking Barrerls

RATING: 10/10

This Western Spaghetti movie was the first I ever watched. It was recommended by a friend of mine :). It’s basically a bunch of dudes who screw up a card game and end up in a wild mess with gangsters and stolen guns.

Chappie

RATING: 10/10

One of the BEST action movies I've watched. it’s basically about a robot who learns to think and feel for himself while getting dragged into a bunch of human drama.

Spirit

RATING:1000/10

OMGGGGG! SOOO sad. If you have never cried before, please prepare with 10 boxes of tissues. BEST MOVIE EVER. It’s basically a wild horse fighting to stay free, finding friends along the way, and dealing with humans who keep trying to tame him.

UpTown Girls

RATING: 10/10

SO ICONIC. It’s about a carefree girl who has to grow up fast when she starts taking care of a super serious little kid, and they end up helping each other out.

Diary Of Wimpy Kid

RATING: 9/10

It’s basically Greg dealing with his annoying older brother Rodrick, and somehow their constant fighting turns into them actually getting along (kinda).

National Treasure

RATING: 8.9/10

It’s about a guy who drags his friends into a wild treasure hunt, following secret clues hidden in U.S. history and famous landmarks while trying to stay a step ahead of the people chasing him.

The Lightning Thief

RATING: 9/10

It’s about a kid who finds out he’s a demigod, gets thrown into this whole crazy Greek-god world, and has to travel across the country with his friends to return Zeus’s lightning bolt before everything blows up.

Final Destination

RATING: 100/10

It’s about a guy who has has a vision and saves people — but the disaster is a bridge collapse. the twist is that the survivors realize they can only “cheat” Death by taking someone else’s remaining life.

Dune

RATING: 9/10

a little too long BUT,It’s about a guy who ends up on this desert planet full of giant sandworms, crazy politics, and mystical powers, and he slowly realizes he might be the one everyone’s been waiting for.

Leave the World Behind

RATING: 100/10

SUPER GOOD, I recommend this movie to everyone all the time, its on Netflix. It’s about two families stuck together during a mysterious nationwide meltdown, trying to figure out what’s actually happening while everything around them gets sketchier and more chaotic.

In Time

RATING: 9/10

It’s about a future where time is literally money, and this guy suddenly gets a ton of it and ends up running for his life while trying to take down the whole messed-up system.

Ride Your Wave

10000000/10

If you want to feel a little something now and then, please watch this movie. Super emotional, Super good. Standing Novation :,)..It’s about a girl who falls in love with a firefighter, loses him, and then starts seeing his spirit appear in water, helping her heal and find her strength again.
