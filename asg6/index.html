Six Thinking Hats

White Hat

The project idea is clear and well thought out, it focuses on giving digital artists a space to share their sticker designs and users a fun way to express themselves. Some details still need clarification, like how uploads work and whether stickers will be free or paid.

Red Hat

This idea feels really fun and fresh. It’s something I could actually see people using and enjoying, especially artists who want to share their work in a more personal way.

Black Hat

One concern is that there are already a lot of sticker platforms out there, so standing out could be tough. It’ll also take some planning to make sure the site runs smoothly and that artists’ work is protected from being copied or misused.

Yellow Hat

This project has a lot of potential because it gives artists a space to connect with people who love their style. It could also make texting more creative and fun, which would definitely appeal to a lot of users.

Green Hat

Try adding features like letting users mix and match stickers to make their own sets or even create animated ones. Find somehting that will seperate you from your competitors. Maybe make a section where the user can create their own sticker through an added feature.

Blue Hat

The project idea is solid and has a clear purpose. At this stage, it might help to do more creative brainstorming (green hat thinking) to explore ways to stand out and keep users coming back.
